I am a pre-doctoral Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Dr. Mohit Jain and Dr. Nipun Kwatra. I currently work on developing low-cost diagnostic solutions for healthcare

Prior to joining MSR India, I have worked at 2 deep-tech startups as one of their first members, Rephrase.ai where I worked on generating audio and lip-syncing with less amount of data (~20 minutes) and Hyperverge where I built a no-code platform HyperTuring which allows Information Extraction from Structured/Unstructured documents with less amount(~100 samples) of data.

I graduated with a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIITD, Delhi, India in 2019 where I was advised by Dr. Mayank Vatsa and Dr. Richa Singh on the topic of Presentation Attacks on Facial Images. .For more details hit me up on my email.
